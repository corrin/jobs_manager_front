## Writing Guide

Each recipe follows this structure:

1. **When to use** - One line describing the situation
2. **What you'll need** - Prerequisites checklist
3. **Steps** - Numbered actions with explanations
4. **What happens next** - Outcomes and follow-ups
5. **Tips** - Business knowledge and warnings
